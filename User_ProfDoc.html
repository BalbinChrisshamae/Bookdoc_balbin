About Dr Andrea Budol
 Dr. Budol is a compassionate primary care physician, listening to her patients and keeping a keen eye for details, so as to provide the best possible care...
She attended Chuchu University where she graduated magna cum laude with a BS in Biology. She went on to medical school at University of the Philippines. Dr. Andrea completed her internal medicine residency at Jammo School of Medicine at Mount Chuchuness – Dikoalam.

Dr. Budol has had extensive training in managing various medical co-morbidities and teaching patients how to better understand their health. She is invested in making her patients feel understood and developing a trusting relationship.
